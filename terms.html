What Carvline is

Carvline is a snowboard (and skiing) video analysis tool that uses AI to give you feedback on your riding technique. We analyze still frames extracted from your video and return scoring + suggestions.

What it isn't

Carvline is not a substitute for professional coaching, medical advice, or supervised training. Snowboarding is an inherently risky activity. You alone are responsible for your safety. Use Carvline as a supplementary tool, not as the sole basis for technique decisions — especially in dangerous terrain (steeps, off-piste, park features, ice).

The AI provides educated guesses about your technique with approximately 70-85% accuracy for beginner-to-intermediate riders. Some advice may be wrong or off-context. Use your judgment.
